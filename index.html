About Me

Hi, I'm Abdullah Salameh, a passionate Full Stack Developer and Electrical Engineering student at Al Hussein Technical University (HTU). I started learning React and front-end development at the age of 15, and since then, I've been dedicated to building dynamic web applications using technologies like React, Node.js, and MongoDB.


Beyond development, I have a deep interest in robotics, working on projects involving Arduino, sensors, and real-time control systems. I enjoy problem-solving and constantly learning new technologies.


In my free time, you'll find me experimenting with hardware, exploring open-source projects, or collaborating with teams to bring innovative ideas to life. Whether it's creating efficient software solutions or building complex systems, I'm always excited to take on new challenges.
